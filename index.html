About Me

I received the B.Sc. degree in the school of management from Xi’an Jiaotong University, China, in 2002.

Then I received the M.Sc. and Ph.D. degrees in computer science from Nanjing University, China, in 2008 and 2011, respectively. My advisor is Professor Daoxu Chen （陈道蓄） and Professor Qing Gu （顾庆）.

Now I am an associate professor in the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Nantong University.

I am a member of ACM, IEEE, and senior member of CCF (China Computer Federation).

Research Interests

My research interests include:

  • Software Defect Prediction 软件缺陷预测

  • Software Fault Localization 软件缺陷定位

  • Regression Testing 回归测试

  • Automatic Program Repair 自动程序修复

  • Combinatorial Testing 组合测试

  • Mutation Testing 变异测试

  • Software Effort Estimation 软件工作量估算

More research information by visiting our smartSE group.
