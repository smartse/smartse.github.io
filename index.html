About Me

I received the B.Sc. degree in the School of Management from Xi’an Jiaotong University, China, in 2002.

Then I received the M.Sc. and Ph.D. degrees in the Department of Computer Science and Technology from Nanjing University, China, in 2008 and 2011, respectively. My advisor is Professor Daoxu Chen （陈道蓄） and Professor Qing Gu （顾庆）.

Now I am an associate professor in the School of Computer Science and Technology at Nantong University.

I am a member of ACM, IEEE and senior member of CCF (China Computer Federation).

Research Interests

My research interests include empirical software engineering, mining software repository, software testing and software maintenance:

  • Software Defect Prediction (Literature Repository, maintenanced by Xiang Chen)

  • Software Fault Localization

  • Regression Testing

  • Automatic Program Repair （Literautre Repository）

  • Combinatorial Testing （Literature Repository）

  • Mutation Testing

  • Stack Overflow Mining

  • Software Effort Estimation

  • Knowledge Graph and its Application on Intelligent Software Engineering

  • Code Embedding

More research information can be found by visiting our smartSE group （智能软件工程研究组）.
